Core platform

A creative toolchain built like a premium film studio

The interface is organized around the actual workflow: upload, analyze, stylize, preview, edit, and export. The product surface below is designed as a production system, not a landing page.

AI video stylization

Stable Video Diffusion, AnimateDiff, ControlNet, and temporal coherence passes for frame-consistent transformation.

Motion preservation

Optical flow, camera trajectory locking, and interpolation keep movement, timing, and lip sync aligned.

Real-time previews

Low-latency frame previews and scene slices let creators steer the result before a full render queue burn.

4K social export

Export MP4, MOV, GIF, alpha video, and aspect-ratio presets for all major social channels.

Masking and layers

Subject masks, brush refinements, background replacement, and layered style regions.

Prompt guidance

Text prompts and reference images steer art direction without losing the motion signature of the source footage.

Queue and autosave

Every action is versioned, autosaved, and synchronized to the render queue with status-aware feedback.

Processing flow

A nine-step AI pipeline with visible state at every stage

This flow mirrors the product experience from upload to export. The UI exposes the processing state so creators feel in control of the render lifecycle.

FAQ

Practical answers for production use

How does motion consistency work?

Motion vectors, optical flow, and temporal coherence models keep object movement, camera paths, and lip sync stable across stylized frames.

Can I mix styles?

Yes. Each scene can carry a unique style and intensity, and the editor supports crossfade blending between styles for hybrid mixed-media results.

What exports are supported?

MP4, MOV, GIF, alpha channel video, and social presets such as 9:16, 1:1, and 16:9 at 720p, 1080p, or 4K.

Can teams collaborate?

Studio and Enterprise include shared workspaces, render status visibility, asset libraries, comments, and multi-user review flows.
